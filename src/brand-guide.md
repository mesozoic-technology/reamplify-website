## Logo Usage

### Files Available
- `logo.svg` - Full logo with background
- `logo-icon-only.svg` - Lightning bolt only (transparent)

### Logo Guidelines
- Minimum clear space: 20% of logo height on all sides
- Minimum display size: 48px Ã— 48px
- Do not distort or skew the logo
- Maintain original aspect ratio when scaling
- Do not add effects or shadows (except elevation)

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Inter** - Modern, clean, highly legible
- Headings: 600-700 weight
- Body: 400-500 weight
- Use for UI, web, and digital applications

### Secondary Font
**Playfair Display** - Elegant, sophisticated
- Use for editorial content and premium contexts
- Pairs well with Inter for contrast

### Monospace Font
**JetBrains Mono** - Technical, modern
- Use for code, data, and technical content

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Primary Usage
- Electric Mint (#5EFFD3): CTAs, links, highlights, logo
- Slate Navy (#3d4f5c): Headers, dark backgrounds, text on light

### Accent Colors
Use sparingly to add energy and visual interest:
- Coral for passion/energy
- Amber for optimism/warmth
- Violet for creativity/innovation

### Text Colors
- Primary text: Gray 900 (#111827)
- Secondary text: Gray 600 (#4B5563)
- Tertiary text: Gray 500 (#6B7280)
